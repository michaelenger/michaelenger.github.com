Projects

NO — A Game of Numbers

A nonogram is a logic puzzle invented in the 80s by the Japanese. It's like Sudoku, only not the same, and now you can play an infinite amount of randomly generated puzzles.

Giant Bomb Recommengine

Giant Bomb is a giant website about video games and this app uses its massive repository of user-generated content to provide you with suggestions for games based on one you already like.

Spotify Flickrstream

While listening to music on Spotify you may want a steady stream of pictures from Flickr based on the current artist. That's what this app does.

Note that the Flickr users are a strange bunch, so the quality of pictures may vary.

RPG Dice Bag

If you play tabletop role-playing games and have an internet-enabled mobile telephone, you may want to check out this little app which lets you roll all sorts of dice to accomodate your insatiable lust for loot and the blood of monsters.
