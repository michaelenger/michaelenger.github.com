Projects

NO — A Game of Numbers

A nonogram is a logic puzzle much like Sudoku. This spectacular web app lets you play an infinite amount of randomly generated puzzles for an infinite amount of time. It's built on top of the Crafty HTML5 game engine and is based on an older game of my own creation.

Giant Bomb Recommengine

Giant Bomb is a giant website about video games and now you can search for suggested games based on one you already like. This app comes to you thanks to Angular and Bootstrap.

Spotify Flickrstream

While listening to music on Spotify you may want a steady stream of pictures from Flickr based on the current artist, so this vanilla JavaScript apps has you covered. You'll of course need Spotify to run the app.

RPG Dice Bag

If you play tabletop role-playing games and have an internet-enabled mobile telephone, you can now roll all sorts of dice to accommodate your insatiable lust for loot and the blood of monsters. The app is made using vanilla JavaScript and is based off my d20.js library. It may also look strange on large non-mobile screens.
